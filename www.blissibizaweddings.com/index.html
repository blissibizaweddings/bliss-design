About Bliss

Considered storytelling with an island soul

Led by creative director Megan Rose, Bliss Ibiza Weddings unites design, production and guest experience under one atelier. Our studio translates your personalities into immersive celebrations that feel both modern and timeless.

Design & Styling House

From colour studies to custom furniture, we craft rooms and rituals that mirror the mood of Ibiza’s landscapes. Each space is plotted with curated art, lighting and tactile details for a cinematic reveal.

Production & Logistics

Our bilingual producers orchestrate every moving part with discretion—from travel manifests and villa takeovers to 4am teardown schedules—so you can move through the weekend in total ease.

Our Approach

A four-part journey to effortless celebrations

Our process is intentionally immersive, giving you space to dream while we translate every detail into a polished, on-island reality.

  1. 01 • Dream

    Discover & align

    We begin with vision sessions, mood exploration and venue scouting to unearth the soul of your celebration.

  2. 02 • Design

    Concept & create

    Our designers craft detailed lookbooks, layouts and renderings while the production team builds budgets and roadmaps.

  3. 03 • Produce

    Build & orchestrate

    We manage partners, logistics and guest communications, shaping every touchpoint to feel fluid and generous.

  4. 04 • Celebrate

    Host & refine

    On the weekend, we direct the show so you can be fully present—handling styling flips, timeline pivots and final farewells.
